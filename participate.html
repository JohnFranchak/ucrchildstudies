Get involved!

If you are interested in being contacted about future studies, the next step is to fill out the participation form. After we receive your information, one of the participating labs will contact you when your child is the right age for an ongoing study. If we are not conducting any studies for your child's age range, you might not hear from us for a while.

Filling out the participation form does not mean that you are obligated to participate in a study. Every time you are contacted about a study, the researcher will describe what will happen during the visit. It's up to you to decide if your child might like to participate. Please visit our FAQ for more information.
