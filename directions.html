Location

Studies take place in our child-friendly labs on the UC Riverside campus. The Psychology Department is located on West Campus Drive adjacent to Parking Lot 6. A researcher will greet you in the parking lot and bring you and your child to the study location.

Parking Information

When you pull into Lot 6, take an immediate left and proceed to the parking spots closest to the Psychology building (near the handicap spots). There will be a parking spot specifically reserved that says "Psychology Clients". You will need a parking permit - please arrange this ahead of your visit with the person who scheduled your appointment.
